We are registered with Medicare and the Psychology Board of Australia and have been trained in the provision of evidence-based therapeutic approaches, such as cognitive-behavioural therapy, interpersonal therapy, psychoeducation, skills training and relaxation strategies.

Our work is culturally informed and gender-affirming. We welcome all referrals, including adults, adolescents, children and families, couples, culturally and linguistically diverse groups, Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islanders, and the LGBTIQA+ community.
