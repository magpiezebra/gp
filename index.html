Our practice

Good Psychology is an alliance of clinical psychologists with a passion for psychoanalytic psychotherapy in its most radical and soulful forms.

Our work is culturally informed and gender-affirming. We welcome all referrals, including adults and adolescents. We’re registered with Medicare and have been trained in the provision of evidence-based therapies.

Our approach

Therapy often focuses on the short-term management of symptoms. Our practice is rooted in a different tradition. We believe in the value of in-depth, long-term exploration for lasting change.
