Bilal, Danu, and Stephen are clinical psychologists with a shared passion for psychoanalytic psychotherapy in its most radical and soulful forms. They met while teaching at the Australian National University.

We are unconditionally registered with the Psychology Board of Australia and trained in the provision of evidence-supported therapeutic approaches such as cognitive-behavioural therapy, interpersonal therapy, psychoeducation, skills training and relaxation strategies.
