Our team

We are Medicare-registered psychologists holding unconditional registration with the Psychology Board of Australia. We are trained in the provision of evidence-based psychological therapies including cognitive-behavioural therapy, interpersonal therapy, psychoeducation, skills training and relaxation strategies.

Our work is culturally informed and gender-affirming.

We welcome all referrals, including adults, adolescents, children and families, couples, culturally and linguistically diverse groups, Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islanders, First Nations people, and the LGBTIQ+ community.

If you would like to speak with any of us, please email for a 24h call-back.
